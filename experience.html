The Ideal Parisian Day

A journey through the City of Light, from sunrise at the Iron Lady to sunset on the water.

  1. The Eiffel Tower at Dawn

    • Watch the sunrise hit the iron lattice.
    • Beat the massive crowds of tourists.
    • Perfect lighting for photography.

  2. Picnic in the Parisian Parks

    • Enjoy fresh baguettes and brie from a local boulangerie.
    • Relax in the iconic green metal chairs of the Tuileries.
    • People-watching at its absolute finest.

  3. The Rue Crémieux at Sunset

    • The "Golden Hour" reflects beautifully off the buildings.
    • Wonderful golden hour pictures.
    • The city lights begin to twinkle as the sun dips.
